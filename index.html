Our Mission

  • To nurture young minds utilizing state-of-the-arts educational tools and methodology.

  • To mold the character of the student according to the highest Islamic moral standards.

  • To motivate the next generation of Muslims to be leaders and affluent members of society.

Our Methodology Based on the 3 T(s) principles.

T

TALEEM

To educate children utilizing their sensory skills (seeing, hearing, touching) and other interactive tools.

T

TARBIYAH

To have faculty and staff who embody the highest Islamic values who teach children by example.

T

TAZKIYA

To promote spiritual as well as physical well-being by focusing on proper Islamic teaching.
