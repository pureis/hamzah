Tuition and Registration

Scholarship

Get involved and be part of this great cause to get immense reward from Allah (SWT). Helping with your tax dollars to support Hamzah Academy is a great way to do so. There are many ways to show your support and involvement by forwarding your tax dollars to Hamzah Academy through GASSO and GLF.


GLF

The Georgia Learning Foundation, Inc. is a non-profit corporation incorporated under the laws of the State of Georgia; is recognized by the Internal Revenue Service as a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization; and is recognized by the Georgia Department of Education as a qualified student scholarship organization under Georgia law. For every dollar that a Georgia taxpayer (individual and businesses) donates to this program, they receive a dollar-for-dollar credit.
http://www.galearn.org/hamzahacademy


GASSO

Georgia Student Scholarship Organization, Inc. is one of the oldest and largest non-profit corporations approved by the State of Georgia to accept and manage tax-credit donations that give Georgia children access to the educational choices they deserve and need. As an SSO authorized by the Georgia Department of Education, GASSO receives and processes donations from individuals, families and corporations, and disburses the funds to designated private schools.
http://www.georgiasso.us/DonationForm.html
