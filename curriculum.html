About Us

Our Curriculum

Hamzah Academy is an elementary school which is committed to providing a dual learning program of excellence in secular and Islamic Studies. Our curriculum is exciting, innovative, stimulating and is designed around our 3 T Methodology.

TA-LEEM: to educate children by engaging them at all learning levels and abilities, utilizing all of their sensory skills, and providing the most up-to-date curriculum and classroom technology available.

TAR-BIYAH: to develop all students by having faculty and staff who nurture the highest Islamic values and constantly teach children, by example, how to be strong Muslims and community members.

TAZKIYA: to purify the hearts and minds of students by promoting spiritual as well as physical wellbeing through a foundation of proper Islamic values.

Your child will learn core subjects such as Math, Language Arts, Social Studies, Science, Art and Physical Education and develop fundamental skills. Both English and Arabic language skills are taught from our Pre-K Program to Fifth Grade through spelling, writing, and reading. Quran is taught as a separate subject with an emphasis on both memorization and reading. Islamic Studies focuses on duas, our prophets and Islamic theology. Our teachers observe the progress of your child and mold their teaching appropriate to his/her learning style and progress.
